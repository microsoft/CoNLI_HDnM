And considering the 27 - year - old yorkshireman finished joint 12th following a closing round of 68 on the blue monster, perhaps it is not too fanciful to suggest willett could become the first masters debutant to slip on the green jacket since fuzzy zoeller in 1979. ` i've always said if you're not going to go to an event and be fully prepared to try to win, there is no point going,' willett said. Danny willett is aiming to become the first debutant to win the masters since fuzzy zoeller in 1979. Willett insists he is preparing to emerge victorious in the first major of 2015.
