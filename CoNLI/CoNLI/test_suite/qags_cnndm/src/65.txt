An entire herd of buffalo has been shot dead by law enforcement in upstate new york after they escaped from a farm. Around 15 animals ran away from their pen in schodack in rensselaer county on thursday evening and then swam across the hudson river. They then started running down the thruway, a series of toll roads which link up the major cities in the state, including buffalo and new york city.
