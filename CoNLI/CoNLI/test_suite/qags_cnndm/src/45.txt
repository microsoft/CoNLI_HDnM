A teenage farmer who struggled with anorexia died after she took an overdose of an animal medication. Charlotte cobbald, 17, took the deadly medicine, which her father stephen was keeping illegally, and injected it after saying she felt like a `failure'. She then ran away from her father, saying `let me die', before she collapsed in a field and her family called 999, an inquest heard. Paramedics fought to save her and took her in an ambulance to hospital, but charlotte died from the effects of the drug.
