A woman is in a critical condition after falling 30 metres from the highest lookout in blue mountains when she slipped trying to retrieve her mobile phone. The 44 - year - old, named by locals as donna christie, slipped and fell from hassans fall lookout at lithgow on tuesday afternoon, the abc reports. She was bushwalking with a male friend and her two children, aged 12 and 13, when she dropped her phone at the lookout, which is almost 1, 100 metres above sea level.
