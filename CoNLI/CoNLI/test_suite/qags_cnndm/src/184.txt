Tennis champion novak djokovic has apologised to a startled ball boy and his parents after the serbian player startled him during his final showdown with andy murray. Djokovic started shouting at his backroom team after he lost the second set of the miami open to murray. During the tirade, djokovic grabbed a towel from the shocked youngster who was caught up in the crossfire. Umpire damien dumusois issued djokovic a warning for the way he shouted at his support team, but there was no reprimand for the manner in which he treated the ball boy.
