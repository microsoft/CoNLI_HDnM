A tory councillor with a history of odd behaviour was told to put down his knife and fork after being caught tucking into a roast duck dinner during a council meeting. Jonny bucknell, 58, was enjoying his meal in the council chamber when a labour rival, theo blackwell, spotted him and alerted other councillors. He was forced to put down his cutlery when the mayor, lazzaro pietragnoli, interrupted the proceedings to tell him off. Taking a stand : jonny bucknell is no stranger to odd behaviour. In 2013 he slept in his car at the tory party conference. He now says he wants a rule change so he can eat a roast dinner at council meetings. The mayor, who was chairing the meeting of camden council in north london, reminded the hungry councillor that eating was banned in the chamber. But the angry diner claims he was unaware eating there was forbidden and said he now aims to campaign for a rule change.
