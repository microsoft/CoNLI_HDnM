Amanjena resort amanjena ( peaceful paradise ) is the first aman resort on the african continent and is located just outside the ancient city of marrakech. Set within an oasis of palms and olive trees, moorish heritage is reflected throughout the pise - walled resort, including the morocco luxury pavilions that radiate out from an ancient bassin. The name amanjena translates as peaceful paradise, which is echoed by its remote, private surroundings.
