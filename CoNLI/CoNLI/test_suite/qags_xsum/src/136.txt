Oscar-winning actor matthew mcconaughey has surprised students at the university of texas at austin by taking part in a scheme to provide them with safe late-night lifts home. On monday the austin resident used a golf buggy to pick some of them up. The sure walk project has been set up to ensure students get back safely from campus when it is dark. Mcconaughey, 47, graduated from the university in 1993. He is an avid fan of its american football team.
